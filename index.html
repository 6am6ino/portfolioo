About Me

Juma Musima, J.M.

I am a licensed attorney with 8+ years of experience in corporate law, intellectual property, and legal tech, combined with full-stack development expertise specializing in Django, JavaScript, and database architecture.

My unique background enables me to bridge the gap between legal requirements and technological solutions, creating efficient digital tools that address specific legal challenges and streamline operations for law firms and legal departments.

After graduating from Harvard Law School and practicing at a top-tier firm, I pursued my passion for technology through a software engineering bootcamp and now specialize in creating custom legal tech solutions that combine my expertise in both fields.
